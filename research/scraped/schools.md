## Why Schools Partner with Track Academy
Track Academy is a **development partner** that helps schools deliver **personal development, leadership, inclusion, and progression** — with optional PE support alongside your existing staff.

### Three pillars:
1. **Character & Leadership** — "Future Leaders Through Sport" sessions linked to Ofsted Personal Development: teamwork, confidence, responsibility.
2. **Inclusion & Access** — Students at partner schools and all pupils on Pupil Premium receive Partnership Bursary Access — fully funded through school partnership.
3. **Pathways to Excellence** — Talent ID days, competition opportunities, progression into Track Academy squads.

## What We Deliver (Primary & Secondary)
- Character & Leadership blocks with mentoring check-ins
- After-school or lunchtime athletics clubs (movement literacy -> event skills)
- Staff CPD and shadowing (optional)
- Athlete Passports: simple tracking of attendance, confidence, fitness and behaviour
- Competition & showcase events hosted with Track Academy
- Optional PE support that complements existing teachers

## Funding model
**All pupils on Pupil Premium at your school get free access to Track Academy programmes.**
- Pupil Premium — target inclusion, attendance, behaviour and mentoring
- PE & Sport Premium (Primary) — strengthen PE quality, extend sport offer, staff CPD

### Why this passes the SLT test
- Clear outcomes tracked via Athlete Passports
- Termly impact reports ready for SLT and Ofsted
- Admin-light — they schedule, deliver, collect data; school approves pupil list
- Safeguarding first: DBS-checked, insured coaches; risk assessments
- Fair access: free places for Pupil Premium; support plans for highlighted children
- Value for money: predictable annual tiers with measurable impact

### How it works (3 steps)
1. Identify Pupil Premium pupils and preferred day/time
2. We deliver sessions and capture Passport data each week
3. Review termly impact report and plan next block

## Partnership Tiers

### Bronze — Community Partner — £1,950/year
- 1 after-school club per week (primary or secondary)
- Free places prioritised for Pupil Premium pupils
- Invitation to local meets & community events
- Basic impact summary each term

### Silver — Development Partner — £3,950/year
- Everything in Bronze
- Character & Leadership block (KS2-KS4)
- Athlete Passports (attendance, confidence, fitness, behaviour)
- Staff CPD / teacher shadowing (1 per term)
- Termly impact report for SLT/Ofsted

### Gold — Legacy Partner — £6,950/year
- Everything in Silver
- Talent ID day & squad progression pathway
- Hosted competition / showcase on site
- Priority bursary access for disadvantaged pupils
- Branded recognition as a Track Academy Partner School

*Prices are per school, per year. Multi-school MAT or cluster packages available on request.*
