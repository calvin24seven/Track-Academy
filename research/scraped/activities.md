# Track Academy — Activities

**URL:** https://trackacademy.co.uk/activities/

## Athletics
Trains at Willesden Sports Centre three times a week:
- **Tuesdays and Thursdays: 5pm - 6.30pm**
- **Saturdays: 11am - 12.30pm**

Training programme open to young people aged **10-21 years old**:
- **Free** if on any financial support
- **£25 a term** otherwise
- Focus on three disciplines: running, jumping, throwing
- Encourage student-athletes to attend meets across the country
- Host own indoor and outdoor competitions each year

## Multi-skills
For younger athletes aged **5-10**:
- **Tuesdays and Thursdays: 4pm - 5pm**
- **Saturdays: 10am - 11am**
- Learning basics of athletics through fun activities
- Develop skills including teamwork and leadership
- **Free** if on financial support or **£25 a term**
