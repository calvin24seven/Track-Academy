## About Us (LinkedIn bio)
We use sport for social change. Charity number 1164222. Track Academy blends athletics, education and mentoring to empower young people (5-21) in under-resourced NW London communities. Based in Brent, where child poverty is 41%, we help young people overcome barriers, build confidence, and develop the skills and networks to shape their own futures. 90% of participants leave with resilience, practical skills and a trusted network - the ultimate launchpad for shaping their education, careers and communities.

### Post: Trustee recruitment (4 months ago)
> Final call: we're looking for one more Trustee. "One trusted adult changes everything." Research shows that a trusted adult can dramatically mitigate the negative impacts of childhood adversity. It's a protective factor that changes life trajectories. But in Brent, young people are twice as likely to lack that emotional support and they're falling through gaps in overstretched systems. We need a new Trustee with direct, hands-on experience in youth work, education or mentoring. Someone who understands the realities of helping young people build confidence, resilience and skills.
