## England Athletics URN
To compete at licensed competitions under Track Academy, athletes need an active England Athletics registration and a URN (Unique Registration Number).

### Who needs a URN?
- Athletes who want to compete under Track Academy
- Athletes entering open meetings, leagues, relays or championships
- Athletes selected for club teams where EA registration is required

### 3-step process
1. Complete the Competing Membership Form
2. Join Spond & make payment (membership + EA registration)
3. We register you with England Athletics and confirm your URN

## Competition sign-ups via Spond
### Club-selected fixtures (leagues/relays)
- Publish fixture details -> Athletes register interest via Spond -> Coaches confirm selections -> Share call times, kit, meet-point

### Open meetings (self-entry)
- Enter directly using organiser's entry link
- Use Track Academy as club (and URN if required)
- Ask coaches for event suggestions and race planning

## Competition day checklist
- Club vest / kit (plus layers)
- Spikes or trainers
- Water + light snacks
- Any required medication (e.g. inhaler)
- Arrive early for warm-up and registration

## Competing Club Membership Form
Fields: First Name, Last Name, DOB, Gender, Athlete email, Parent/Carer name, Parent/Carer email, mobile, Address (full), Country, School (optional), Main event group (Sprints/Middle Distance/Throws/Jumps/Multi Events/Multi Skills 5-10), Member of another club?, Existing EA URN?

Consent checkboxes:
- Consent to EA registration/renewal
- Consent to be contacted about competitions, fixtures, updates
