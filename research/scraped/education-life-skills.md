# Track Academy — Education & Life Skills

**URL:** https://trackacademy.co.uk/education-life-skills/

## Education
The Education and Life Skills programmes are the core curriculum that empowers young people with the tools to thrive — not just as athletes, but as confident, resilient individuals ready for education, employment and life.

### Academic support
- Stable, encouraging environment for academic growth
- Weekly sessions in core subjects: Maths, English, Science
- Helps close attainment gaps and build academic confidence

### Session times
- **Tuesday 4pm - 5pm: Study Support** — help with subjects from basic coursework to GCSE level
- **Saturday 9am - 11am: Tuition** — for both multi-skills (5-10) and mainstream (10-21) groups; focus on challenging topics, build confidence for independent work

**No added charge for educational support.**

## Life Skills — 10 Core Skills
1. **Listening** — listening and repeating exercises to build attention and confidence
2. **Presenting** — presenting yourself to staff (name, school, reason for training, plans, gains)
3. **Networking & social skills** — live networking events, asking staff questions
4. **Problem solving** — plan an event with £500,000 budget in New York, 30min brainstorm, 5min presentation
5. **Creativity** — picking words from magazines, forming stories
6. **Staying positive** — writing 2 things they enjoy about athletics and a quote about Track Academy
7. **Aiming high** — writing down goals for the year/season
8. **Discipline** — "say no" to favourite words/habits (e.g. "it's hard", "I can't do it")
9. **Teamwork** — resolving fake corporate complaints in teams (easy, medium, hard)
10. **Leadership** — survival scenario game (Alaska, 1 week, choose 10 items from 25)

### Knowledge and employability
- Regular sessions for older athletes: CV writing, nutrition, finance, time management
- Special guest speakers
- Visits to businesses across London
